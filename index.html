中文版--隐私政策

本隐私政策适用于由 Auto Sound Recorder AI（以下简称“服务提供商”、“我们”）提供的移动应用程序 Auto Sound Recorder AI（以下简称“本应用”）。本应用作为一项商业服务按“现状”提供。

1. 麦克风权限与用途

本应用需要访问您设备的麦克风，以实现录音功能，并在设备本地检测人声和环境声音强度，从而在满足设定条件时自动启动录音。麦克风权限仅用于以下目的：

  • 手动录音。
  • 本地人声识别。
  • 本地音量阈值检测。
  • 自动触发录音。

2. 本地 AI / 本地音频分析

本应用使用设备本地的算法或 AI 模型，对麦克风输入的音频进行实时分析，以识别人声并结合音量阈值判断是否自动开始录音。相关分析仅在您的设备本地完成，不会将音频上传到云端进行 AI 处理。

3. 不上传云端 / 不做语音转文字

本应用不会将您的录音内容、实时麦克风音频或本地人声识别分析结果上传到云端服务器进行 AI 处理。本应用不提供语音转文字（Speech-to-Text）功能，也不会将录音转换为文本内容。

4. 信息收集和使用

除实现应用功能所必需的本地处理外，本应用不会主动收集您的录音内容用于云端识别、转写或第三方分析。如果应用在运行过程中产生必要的基础技术信息，这些信息仅用于应用正常运行、故障排查和改进体验，且不会用于云端语音识别或自动录音触发分析。

5. 第三方服务

本应用不使用第三方服务来处理您的录音内容、麦克风音频或本地 AI 分析结果。如您的设备或系统组件提供基础功能支持，相关处理仅限于设备或系统层面的必要运行，不用于将您的音频上传到任何第三方云服务。

6. 数据共享

除法律法规要求、保护用户或公众安全、调查欺诈或回应合法政府请求外，我们不会出售您的个人信息，也不会向第三方共享用于本地人声识别或自动录音触发的音频内容。

7. 数据保留

如果本应用在运行过程中产生必要的基础技术信息，这些信息仅在实现应用功能、维护应用和排查问题所必需的时间内保留。如果您希望删除您通过本应用提供的个人信息，请通过下方联系方式与我们联系。

8. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

9. 安全

我们重视信息安全，并采取合理的技术和组织措施保护我们处理的信息。

10. 隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的版本将在本页面发布，并自发布之日起生效。

English version--Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Auto Sound Recorder AI (the “App”) provided by Auto Sound Recorder AI (the “service provider,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). The App is provided as a commercial service on an “AS IS” basis.

1. Microphone permission and purpose

The App requires access to your device’s microphone to enable recording and to perform on-device voice and sound-level detection so that recording can start automatically when preset conditions are met. Microphone access is used only for the following purposes:

  • Manual recording
  • On-device voice detection
  • On-device sound threshold detection
  • Automatic recording triggers

2. On-device AI / local audio analysis

The App uses on-device algorithms or AI models to analyze microphone input in real time in order to detect human speech and determine whether automatic recording should begin based on configured sound thresholds. This analysis is performed locally on your device only. Audio is not sent to the cloud for AI processing.

3. No cloud upload / no speech-to-text

The App does not upload your recordings, live microphone audio, or on-device voice analysis results to cloud servers for AI processing. The App does not provide speech-to-text transcription and does not convert recordings into text.

4. Information collection and use

Except for limited local processing required to provide the App’s core functionality, the App does not actively collect your recordings for cloud recognition, transcription, or third-party analysis. If the App generates necessary basic technical information during operation, such information is used only for normal operation, troubleshooting, and improving the App experience, and is not used for cloud voice recognition or automatic recording trigger analysis.

5. Third-party services

The App does not use third-party services to process your recordings, microphone audio, or on-device AI analysis results. If your device or system components provide basic functionality support, such processing is limited to what is necessary for device or system operation and is not used to upload your audio to any third-party cloud service.

6. Data sharing

Except where required by law, necessary to protect users or the public, investigate fraud, or respond to lawful government requests, we do not sell your personal information and do not share audio content used for on-device voice detection or automatic recording triggers with third parties.

7. Data retention

If the App generates necessary basic technical information during operation, such information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain the App, and resolve issues. If you would like to request deletion of personal information you may have provided, please contact us using the email below.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Security

We value information security and take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we process.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and become effective upon posting.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how the App handles data, you may contact us at:
yaoweidev@outlook.com
